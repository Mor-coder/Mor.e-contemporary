  • Mor.e Contemporary is a collaboration between myself, Mor, and a variety of artists working with different media. And it goes approximately like this:

  • Get 3/4 of artist's creativity and mix it in a BIG bowl with talent, ego, and 35ml of painful attention to details;
  • Pour an ounce of empathy and zen attitude into the bowl;
  • Add two ounces of thinking out of the box attitude;
  • Put hours of computer working into a cocktail shaker;
  • Breath in, breath out. You got this! No, I don't. Yes, you do!
  • Add more hours checking is all good to go to print (this is at your discretion);
  • Clear the mess in your head. You seriously got this, grl!
  • Pour the cocktail mix into days of installing and curating;
  • It looks f***ing wicked! OMG, should we swap that for that? Did we not take that work?!
  • Add the months of chatting, mindmapping, and researching previously mixed together in a separate bowl.
  • It's all good, we got them all. Happy time. Where is my glass of wine?
  • Jump three times on your left foot, close your eyes hoping there won't be any major kerfuffle
  • Take a massive jar and pour all the ingredients together. Let it rest for a minute.
  • Big hugs with the artists and everyone involved
  • That was fun, let's do it again!
