Mor.e contemporary is a cocktail obtained by the mixing and stirring of the following ingredients:

  • A good dose of artist's creativity, talent, and ego;
  • An ounce of good will to get stuff done;
  • Two ounces of thinking out of the box attitude;
  • Hours of computer working to produce all the digital stuff;
  • More hours checking is all good to go to print;
  • Days of installing and curating;
  • Lastly, months of chatting, mindmapping, and researching between the artist and Mor.


    • The ingredients can be mixed in a different order and the quantities vary from time to time. It is 100% sure that the outcome will have a unique taste, diverse from the previous one, every time it is made. It's ok, ultimately there is no right or wrong answer, just different interpretations.
