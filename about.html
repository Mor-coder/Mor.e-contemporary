Mor.e Contemporary emerged from Mor's aspiration to champion and advance the creative pursuits of her circle of artist friends. She envisioned establishing a platform where sincere and evaluative discussions could cultivate fresh concepts, provoke thought, and ultimately inspire innovative endeavors. Rooted in her conviction that art serves as a lens to comprehend our complex world, Mor's dedication to deciphering messages for wider accessibility, coupled with her intrigue for diverse media, has guided her collaborations with artists employing various mediums to convey distinct messages.
