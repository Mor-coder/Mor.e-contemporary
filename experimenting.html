"Arcade Mor" by Tuktu and the Belugas Quartet. Video courtesy of the artist.


Experimenting with Tuktu and the Belugas, or, in other words, be their canvas for approximately 8 hours straight while they made their magic, was a fun and painful experience. The process itself took about 8 hours of straight focus on their behalf and complete stillness on mine (that was the painful bit). Once the visual has been done, the music has been produced and adjusted to create a trippy animation that, little we knew at the time, would have become pretty popular in the music industry. Since then, Tuktu and the Belugas have been asked to create set for music videos in the same fashion, including painting the band.
